Our Story

Founded in 2023, Usagi Coffee began as a small passion project between friends who believed in creating more than just coffee. We dreamed of a place where stories are shared, memories are made, and every cup brings warmth and connection.

Our beans are handpicked from the finest local farms, roasted with precision, and brewed with heart. Every detail – from our carefully crafted menu to the cozy ambiance – reflects our commitment to quality and creativity.

More than just a café, Usagi Coffee is a celebration of community and the simple joys of life. Come join our story, one sip at a time.
